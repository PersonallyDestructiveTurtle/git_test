Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Breadding
  • Egg
  • FLour
  • Black pepper

steps

  1. Heat oil til desired temperature
  2. Mix salt, flour, breadding, black pepper
  3. Dip Chicken in egg
  4. Coat chicken in flour mixture
  5. Fry for 15 mins
  6. dry, serve, and enjoy
